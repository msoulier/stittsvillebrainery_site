Introduction to Bullet Journaling

Are you feeling disorganized or overwhelmed by your to-do list? Do you find yourself missing important dates or appointments? If so, this workshop is for you! Join us for a fun and interactive class on Bullet Journaling. Bullet Journaling, also known as Bujo, is a personal organizational system, popularized on Pinterest and Instagram with its eye-catching and often artistic pages. Designed by designer Ryder Carroll, it aims to organize your to-do lists, schedules and reminders into a single resource, a simple notebook. Click here to learn more and register.
